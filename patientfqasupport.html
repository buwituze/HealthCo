Frequently Asked Questions

What is the role of a Performance Tracker?

The Performance Tracker feature is set to help you analyze your waste management performance. Identify areas for improvement and contribute to a cleaner community.

How does Performance Tracker work?

Everytime you complete a waste collection/disposition schedule, EcoCycle automatically updates your performance tracker. This allows you to track your waste collection/disposition and recycling efforts.
